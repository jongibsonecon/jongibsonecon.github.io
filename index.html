About Me

I am a health economist specialising in the allocation of healthcare resources, with a particular focus on the composition of the primary care workforce and the evaluation of health interventions and policies. My work aims to support evidence-based decision-making in national and global health systems. My current work focuses on mental health interventions in low- and middle-income countries (Indonesia).
